Weapons

For weapons, we are going to first attach the sprite to the player, then make the sprite rotate to point to the location of your cursor. First, insert your weapon sprite:
this.weapon = this.physics.add.image(this.player.x, this.player.y, 'weapon')
you can set the scale to whatever you need to.

Then, you would want to make sure that the weapon sticks to the player, thus, we are going to update the weapon sprite to follow the player using:
